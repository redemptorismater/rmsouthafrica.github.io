GALLERIES

ARCHBISHOP'S VISIT

The past 4th of July the Seminary was honoured by the visit of Archbishop Stephen Brislin. This day coincided with the feast of one of the protectors of our seminary Blessed Pier Giorgio Frassati.

MISSION ON THE SQUARES

The Neocatecumenal Way did the mission in the Sundays of Saster in many squares around the world. This also took place in South Africa and the seminarians were part of it.
Locations: Cape Town, Oudshoorn.
